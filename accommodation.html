Accommodation

Edgbaston Park Hotel

We have reserved a number of rooms at the on-campus Edgbaston Park Hotel at the special rate of £112/£125.5 per night for single/double occupancy, with breakfast included. Rooms are available on a first-come first-served basis, so do book early to avoid disappointment.

If you would like to stay additional nights, e.g. the 4th or 8th January, then you can either email us at atomsmoleculesbham@gmail.com or check the box on the registration form, so we can enquire with the hotel and arrange this on your behalf to benefit from the special rate.
