Programme Overview

The main part of the conference will begin around midday on Tuesday 5th Jan, and finish with lunch on Friday 8th January.

The programme will consist of invited talks and contributed talks, as well as a poster session on Wednesday 6th Jan and a conference dinner on Thursday 7th Jan.

You can also opt in to an early-careers social evening on Tuesday 5th Jan and optional lab tours in the afternoon of Friday 8th Jan.

Full information on the programme will be available here in due course.
