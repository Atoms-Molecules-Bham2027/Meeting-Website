Quantum Simulation and Many-Body Physics with Ultracold Atoms and Molecules 5th-8th January 2027 · University of Birmingham

This conference will focus on recent advances in ultracold atoms and molecules, and is jointly organised by the EPSRC programme grants: “Quantum Advantage in Quantitative Quantum Simulation” (QQQS) and “Quantum Many-Body Physics with Ultracold Polar Molecules (QS-Mol)”.
